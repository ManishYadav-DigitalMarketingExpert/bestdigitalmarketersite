FAQ

Website Designing Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before starting your website project with us.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

A standard 5–10 page business website takes 2–3 weeks. A full eCommerce store typically takes 4–6 weeks. Custom or complex websites may take 6–12 weeks. Timelines depend on how quickly you provide content, feedback and approvals. We share a clear project timeline before we begin.

Which platform should I choose — WordPress, Shopify or Wix?

+

It depends on your goals. WordPress is best for corporate, blog and flexible websites. Shopify is ideal if you're selling products online. Wix is great for simple, self-managed sites. Custom builds suit SaaS products and unique requirements. We'll recommend the best platform for your specific needs during your free consultation.

Do you provide domain registration and hosting?

+

Yes — we handle everything. Domain registration across 200+ TLDs (.com, .co.uk, .ai, .tech, .in, .io and more), shared hosting, VPS, business and cloud hosting setup — all configured for speed, security and SEO. You can also use your existing domain and hosting if preferred.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+

Absolutely — every website we build is mobile-first and tested across 20+ device sizes including smartphones, tablets and desktops. Since over 70% of web traffic comes from mobile devices, we prioritise mobile UX in every layout, font size, button placement and interactive element we design.

Will my website be SEO-friendly?

+

Yes — SEO is built into every website we deliver. This includes clean URL structure, optimised meta titles and descriptions, H-tag hierarchy, schema/structured data markup, XML sitemap, robots.txt, image alt tags, canonical URLs, Google Search Console setup and Core Web Vitals optimisation.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

+

Yes — we build websites with easy-to-manage CMS backends. We provide a training session after launch so you can add/edit content, upload blogs, update products and manage pages independently. For WordPress and Wix sites this is especially straightforward. We also offer monthly maintenance packages if you'd rather we handle everything.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

+

Yes. All our packages include a post-launch support period (1–6 months depending on plan). After that, we offer monthly maintenance retainers covering plugin updates, security patches, content edits, performance monitoring, backups and technical support — so your site stays secure and up-to-date.

Can you redesign or improve my existing website?

+

Absolutely. Website redesigns are one of our most common projects. We audit your current site — design, speed, SEO, UX and conversion rate — then rebuild it on the same or a new platform with improved performance, modern design and better business results. We can often migrate all your existing content and preserve your SEO rankings.
