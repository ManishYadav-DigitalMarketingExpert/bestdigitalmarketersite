FAQ

Frequently Asked SEO Questions

Everything you need to know about our SEO services before getting started.

How long does SEO take to show results?

+

SEO is a long-term investment. You'll typically start seeing movement in rankings within 3–6 months, with meaningful traffic and revenue impact appearing at 6–12 months. Highly competitive niches may take longer. We maintain full transparency throughout and share progress reports every month.

Do you guarantee #1 rankings on Google?

+

No ethical SEO agency can guarantee a specific rank — Google's algorithm is dynamic and ever-changing. What we do guarantee is a data-driven strategy, transparent execution, and measurable improvement in rankings, traffic, and conversions. Our track record of 35K+ ranked keywords speaks for itself.

What is the difference between On-Page and Off-Page SEO?

+

On-Page SEO refers to everything you optimize within your website — content, meta tags, headings, internal links, and site structure. Off-Page SEO involves activities outside your site, primarily building high-quality backlinks and digital PR to improve your domain's authority and trustworthiness in Google's eyes.

Do you offer white-label SEO services for agencies?

+

Yes! We offer fully white-labeled SEO services for digital marketing agencies and resellers. You maintain the client relationship while we handle all SEO deliverables under your brand. We provide unbranded reports, dedicated account management, and seamless collaboration at scale.

Will my rankings drop if I stop SEO?

+

Unlike paid ads that stop immediately when you pause, SEO results are more durable. However, competitors continue to optimize, so without ongoing effort, rankings can gradually decline. Consistent SEO work compounds over time — making it the single highest-ROI digital channel long-term.

Do you work with businesses outside India?

+

Absolutely. We provide SEO services across 175+ countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Australia, and more. Our international SEO expertise covers multilingual websites, hreflang implementation, country-specific keyword strategies, and building authority in global markets.

How do you measure SEO success?

+

We track keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, click-through rates, conversion rates, domain authority, backlink profile, and revenue attributed to organic search. You'll get a real-time live dashboard plus a detailed monthly report with clear insights and recommendations.

Can SEO work alongside Google Ads / PPC?

+

Yes — and they work best together. Google Ads gives you instant visibility while SEO builds long-term organic assets. Data from PPC campaigns (winning keywords, ad copy that converts) can directly inform your SEO strategy, and vice versa. We offer both services and can manage them in sync for maximum ROI.
