Conversion Rate Optimization

Turn Store Visitors Into Active Installers

Ranking is only half the battle — your app's store page must convert visitors into installs. We optimize every visual and textual element to maximize your CVR (Conversion Rate from page view to install).

🎨

App Icon Design & Testing

Your icon is the first thing users see — in search results, charts and on their home screen. We design high-impact icons and A/B test multiple variants to find the version that drives the highest CTR and install rate.

📸

Screenshot Design & Sequence

Screenshots 1 and 2 are shown in search results on both stores — they determine whether a user taps through to your listing. We design and sequence screenshots with compelling captions that showcase your app's most valuable features first.

🎬

App Preview Video

Apps with preview videos see 25–35% higher install rates. We advise on or create short (15–30 second) preview videos that show your app in action — demonstrating the core value proposition within the first 3 seconds.

🧪

A/B Testing (Store Listing Experiments)

Both Google Play and Apple App Store offer native A/B testing tools. We systematically test icon variants, screenshot orders, short descriptions and feature graphics — using statistical significance to identify the combination that maximizes your CVR.

⭐

Rating Strategy & Review Generation

We implement in-app review prompts triggered at optimal moments (after a successful user action), craft templates for responding to negative reviews, and establish a process for consistently maintaining 4.5+ star ratings on both stores.

🌐

Localized Store Listing Pages

We create localized store listing pages for your top 5–10 target markets — with translated titles, localized screenshots (showing the UI in local language) and culturally relevant value propositions that convert significantly better than generic English listings.

FAQ

App Store Optimization Questions Answered

What is ASO and how is it different from SEO?

+

ASO (App Store Optimization) is the process of improving your app's visibility and conversion rate in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Like SEO for websites, ASO focuses on keyword rankings — but it also includes visual elements (screenshots, icon), ratings, and conversion rate that SEO doesn't address. The algorithm, keyword volumes and competitive dynamics are also very different from Google web search.

How long does ASO take to show results?

+

You'll typically see initial keyword ranking movements within 2–4 weeks of optimizing your listing. More significant download growth usually follows in weeks 6–12 as rankings consolidate and the algorithm registers improved conversion rates. Localization results in new markets can appear faster — often within 2–3 weeks of going live in a new language.

Do you optimize for both Google Play and Apple App Store?

+

Yes — and both stores require different strategies. Google Play indexes your description like a web page, allowing keyword placement across 4,000 characters. The App Store has a strict 100-character keyword field separate from your description. The ranking signals, A/B testing tools and editorial featuring programmes are also unique to each platform. We apply platform-specific strategies for maximum results on both.

Can ASO reduce my paid User Acquisition (UA) costs?

+

Significantly. Strong ASO reduces your reliance on paid UA by growing your organic download baseline — meaning you get installs for free that you'd otherwise pay $1–$5+ per install for. Additionally, higher organic rankings and a better-converting store page directly improve the efficiency of your paid campaigns, as your app appears more credible to users who click through from ads.

What's included in an ASO audit?

+

Our ASO audit covers: current keyword rankings and coverage gaps, metadata optimization score, screenshot and icon conversion effectiveness, competitor listing analysis, ratings and review sentiment, category selection review, localization opportunities and technical issues (app size, update frequency, crash rate signals). You'll receive a prioritized action plan with specific recommendations for each area.

How do you improve app ratings ethically?

+

We implement in-app review request prompts that trigger at moments of high user satisfaction — for example, after a user completes a key action or reaches a milestone. This dramatically increases the volume of positive reviews while staying fully compliant with both Google Play and Apple App Store policies. We also advise on responding to negative reviews in ways that sometimes result in users updating their rating.

Do you offer ASO for games as well as utility apps?

+

Yes. While game ASO and utility app ASO share the same underlying principles, games have unique dynamics — visual creative plays an even larger role, preview videos are critical, and category competition is fierce. We've optimized listings for both casual and mid-core games, as well as apps across finance, health, education, productivity and e-commerce categories.

Do you offer white-label ASO for agencies?

+

Yes. We provide full white-label ASO services for mobile development agencies, growth agencies and product studios. All deliverables — keyword research, metadata, screenshot guidance and monthly reports — are provided unbranded under your agency name. Contact us for white-label pricing and partnership terms.
