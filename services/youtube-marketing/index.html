YouTube Ads

YouTube Ads That Drive Real Conversions

YouTube Ads reach the right audience at the exact moment they're watching relevant content — with unparalleled targeting, visual storytelling and measurable ROAS that makes YouTube one of the most powerful paid channels available.

▶️

Skippable In-Stream Ads (TrueView)

The most popular YouTube ad format — plays before or during videos and can be skipped after 5 seconds. You only pay when viewers watch 30+ seconds or take an action, making it incredibly cost-efficient for brand awareness and lead generation. We craft the first 5 seconds as pure hook to drive maximum view-through.

🔒

Non-Skippable In-Stream Ads

15–20 second ads that cannot be skipped — guaranteed full message delivery to a targeted audience. Best for brand launches, product announcements and high-impact messaging where getting the complete story in front of your audience is critical. We write tight, punchy scripts that deliver in under 20 seconds.

⚡

Bumper Ads (6 Seconds)

Ultra-short, non-skippable 6-second ads served before videos — perfect for brand recall campaigns alongside longer ad formats. Studies show bumper ads increase brand recall by 47%. We create high-impact, memorable 6-second concepts that stick in your audience's memory long after the video ends.

🔍

Discovery & In-Feed Ads

Sponsored thumbnails that appear in YouTube search results and recommended video feeds — positioned alongside organic content your audience is actively searching for. Drives highly qualified viewers to your channel or landing page when they're already in an active discovery mindset.

🔄

YouTube Remarketing

Retarget website visitors, channel viewers, video engagers and custom audience lists with tailored YouTube ads — re-engaging warm prospects with contextually relevant messaging that drives conversion significantly better than cold audience targeting alone.

🛒

Video Action & Shopping Campaigns

Drive direct conversions from YouTube ads — linking product listings, lead forms and purchase actions directly from the video unit. YouTube Video Action campaigns consistently deliver strong CPAs for eCommerce and lead generation brands, especially when paired with custom intent audiences.

FAQ

YouTube Marketing Questions Answered

How long does it take to grow a YouTube channel?

+

With a consistent content strategy and proper Video SEO, most channels see meaningful momentum within 3–6 months — with significant subscriber and view growth at the 6–12 month mark. YouTube rewards consistency more than any other factor. Channels that combine organic Video SEO with Shorts and cross-promotion typically grow 2–3x faster than those relying on long-form content alone.

Do I need to create the video content myself?

+

We provide the strategy, Video SEO, thumbnails, scripting and channel management — but the on-camera talent or video production is typically provided by you or your team. However, for Shorts and some content types, we can advise on simple, high-performing formats that don't require professional filming equipment. We can also connect you with video production partners if needed.

What's the difference between Video SEO and YouTube Ads?

+

Video SEO (organic) involves optimising your video title, description, tags, thumbnail and watch signals so YouTube ranks and recommends your content to relevant audiences for free. YouTube Ads (paid) are campaigns you pay for to reach specific audiences. Both are complementary — organic builds long-term authority that compounds forever, while ads generate immediate targeted reach. We recommend combining both for maximum channel growth.

How important are YouTube Shorts for channel growth?

+

Extremely important in 2026. YouTube Shorts now generates 70 billion daily views and is the algorithm's primary tool for discovering new creators to promote. The Shorts feed reaches non-subscribers — making it the single most effective way to grow a new audience. Channels that consistently post both Shorts and long-form content grow subscriber bases 3–5x faster than long-form only channels in competitive niches.

What YouTube ad budget do I need to start?

+

YouTube Ads work on a cost-per-view (CPV) model — you typically pay ₹0.50–₹3 per view (or $0.01–$0.05 in international markets). We recommend a minimum monthly budget of ₹30,000–₹50,000 ($400–$600) to gather meaningful data and optimise performance. YouTube Ads can deliver impressively low CPAs compared to other platforms, particularly for awareness and retargeting campaigns.

Can YouTube marketing work for B2B companies?

+

Absolutely — and it's heavily underutilised in B2B. YouTube is the #1 platform where B2B decision-makers research products and services before making a purchase. Long-form educational content, product demos, case studies and thought leadership videos build enormous trust with B2B buyers. Combined with LinkedIn-targeting YouTube Ads, B2B brands consistently achieve strong CPL and pipeline contribution from YouTube.

Do you help with YouTube channel monetisation?

+

Yes. We help channels reach the YouTube Partner Programme (YPP) requirements — 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours in 12 months — as quickly as possible through strategic Video SEO and content acceleration. Once monetised, we also advise on maximising revenue from AdSense, channel memberships, Super Thanks, brand sponsorships and product integrations.

Do you offer white-label YouTube management for agencies?

+

Yes. We provide full white-label YouTube channel management for digital and creative agencies — delivering all strategy documents, Video SEO, thumbnail briefs, analytics reports and growth recommendations under your brand. Your clients see seamless YouTube growth delivered by "your team." Contact us for white-label partnership details and pricing.
