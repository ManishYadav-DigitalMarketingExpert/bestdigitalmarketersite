FAQ

Native Advertising Questions Answered

What is native advertising and how is it different from display ads?

+

Native advertising is paid content that matches the look, feel and function of the media format in which it appears. Unlike display ads (banners), native ads blend into editorial content — appearing as recommended articles, in-feed sponsored posts or content widgets. They're less disruptive, not blocked by ad blockers and generate 3x higher CTR than display banners.

What budget do I need to start native advertising?

+

Most native platforms have a minimum daily spend requirement of ₹1,500–₹5,000/day ($20–$60). We recommend a minimum monthly budget of ₹50,000–₹1,00,000 ($600–$1,200) to gather enough data for optimization. Smaller budgets make it hard to test creative variations and reach statistically significant conclusions in a reasonable timeframe.

Is Taboola or Outbrain better for my campaign?

+

Both are excellent — the right choice depends on your audience, geography and goals. Taboola offers larger scale (1.4B+ users) and is stronger for emerging markets like India and Southeast Asia. Outbrain has a more premium publisher list (CNN, BBC) and works better for premium brand positioning and B2B. We often recommend running both and comparing performance after 30 days.

What makes a native ad creative effective?

+

The single biggest factor is the headline — specifically, curiosity gaps and emotional triggers. "How This Mumbai Family Saved ₹4 Lakhs on Their Home Loan" outperforms "Get a Home Loan Today" every time. Paired with a high-contrast, emotionally resonant thumbnail image and a landing page that delivers on the headline's promise — this combination consistently generates CTRs of 2–5%.

How long does it take to see results from native ads?

+

Native campaigns typically need 2–4 weeks of learning before you see optimized performance. The first week is about finding which creative combinations drive the best CTR. Week 2–3 is about identifying converting audiences and publishers. By week 4, we have enough data to scale winners and cut losers — and you'll see CPL and ROAS trending positively.

Can native advertising work for B2B companies?

+

Absolutely. Microsoft Audience Network is particularly powerful for B2B due to its integration with LinkedIn profile data — allowing targeting by job title, company size and industry. Outbrain also works well for B2B when targeting business and technology publishers. We've run successful B2B native campaigns for SaaS, consulting firms and enterprise technology brands.

Do you manage the landing page as well?

+

We provide landing page recommendations and CRO (conversion rate optimization) guidance as part of all our plans. For Growth and Enterprise clients, we can build dedicated native landing pages or advertorials — editorial-style pages that match the content experience of the publisher site, which consistently outperform standard product pages for native traffic.

Yes — we offer full white-label native advertising management for digital agencies. We manage everything under your brand — campaign setup, creative, optimization and unbranded monthly reports. Your clients see seamless native campaign performance delivered by "your team." Contact us for agency partnership pricing.
