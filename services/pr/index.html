FAQ

Public Relations Questions Answered

How is PR different from advertising?

+

Advertising is paid placement — you pay for space and control the message. PR is earned or managed editorial coverage — a journalist chooses to feature your brand because your story is genuinely newsworthy or interesting to their audience. Earned PR carries far more credibility because it's a third-party endorsement, not a paid promotion. The impact on brand trust is significantly higher.

How long does it take to secure PR placements?

+

Paid editorial placements typically go live within 3–7 business days. Earned media placements take 2–6 weeks depending on the publication's editorial calendar and the strength of the news angle. For urgent announcements, we can execute wire distribution and Google News indexation within 24–48 hours of receiving your brief.

Can you guarantee media placements?

+

For paid editorial and sponsored content, yes — we guarantee placements on agreed publications with specific timelines and live URLs. For earned media (organic journalist coverage), no ethical PR agency can guarantee specific outlets — but our placement success rate across all pitches is consistently 3–5x the industry average, due to our journalist relationships and story quality.

Does PR help with SEO?

+

Significantly — especially digital PR. Editorial links from high-authority news sites (DA 60–90+) are among the most powerful signals for Google rankings. A single link from Economic Times or YourStory can lift your domain authority more than 100 average directory links. We specifically track DA, anchor text and referral traffic for every PR placement to measure SEO impact.

Do you manage crisis PR?

+

Yes. Our crisis PR service activates within hours of a reputational event — with a crisis communication plan, journalist response templates, negative content suppression strategy and positive coverage campaign. We also provide ongoing ORM (Online Reputation Management) to monitor and protect your brand's online image proactively.

What industries do you have PR experience in?

+

We have deep PR experience across luxury fashion, government, technology, education, healthcare, real estate, e-commerce, finance and more. Our client roster includes Delhi Police, GAIL India, JJ Valaya, UC Berkeley Haas, Airalo eSIM and Lenovo — giving us journalist relationships in each of these sectors specifically.

Do you offer international PR coverage?

+

Yes. We run PR campaigns across 175+ countries — with specific expertise in India, UAE (Gulf News, Khaleej Times), UK (Daily Mirror, The Guardian affiliates) and USA (Forbes, Entrepreneur, Inc). We manage multilingual press releases, region-specific journalist outreach and international wire distribution for global brand launches.

Do you offer white-label PR for agencies?

+

Yes. We provide full white-label PR services for marketing and digital agencies who want to offer PR to their clients without building an in-house team. We deliver all placements, press releases and reports unbranded under your agency name — with no client-facing contact from our team.
