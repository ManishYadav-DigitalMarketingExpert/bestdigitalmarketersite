FAQ

Google Ads Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before investing in Google Ads management.

How much budget do I need to start Google Ads?

+

There's no fixed minimum — but we recommend at least ₹30,000–₹50,000/month (or $400–$600) to gather meaningful data and see results. For competitive industries like legal, real estate, or healthcare, a higher budget produces faster outcomes. We'll advise you on the right starting budget during your free consultation.

How soon will I see results from Google Ads?

+

Unlike SEO, Google Ads can generate clicks and conversions from Day 1. However, it typically takes 4–8 weeks of data collection and optimization to reach peak performance. The first month is about learning; from Month 2 onwards, you'll see consistent improvement in ROAS and conversions.

Will I own my Google Ads account?

+

Absolutely — 100%. Your Google Ads account is created in your name and you retain full ownership always. We manage it via Manager Account (MCC) access. If you ever decide to stop working with us, you keep everything — all historical data, campaigns, and optimizations.

What is your management fee structure?

+

We charge a flat monthly management fee (not a percentage of ad spend), so our incentives are always aligned with your ROAS — not with spending more of your money. Our exact fee depends on campaign complexity and ad spend volume. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you manage campaigns for businesses outside India?

+

Yes! We manage Google Ads campaigns across 175+ countries including the USA, UK, UAE, Australia, Singapore, Canada, and more. We have experience with multi-currency billing, international keyword research, and geo-targeted campaign structures for global markets.

What's the difference between Search Ads and P-Max?

+

Search Ads are keyword-triggered text ads that appear on Google Search results — highly intent-driven and precise. Performance Max (P-Max) is Google's newest campaign type that runs across all Google channels (Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, Maps) using AI. Both are powerful — which one is right for you depends on your goals. We often use them together.

Can you take over my existing Google Ads account?

+

Yes, account takeovers are very common. We start with a thorough audit of your existing campaigns, identify what's working and what's wasting budget, and then restructure accordingly. Most clients see significant improvement within the first 30 days of our management.

Do you offer Google Ads + SEO together?

+

Yes — and this combination is incredibly powerful. Google Ads gives you instant visibility while SEO builds long-term organic assets. Data from your Search campaigns (winning keywords, best-converting ad copy) directly informs our SEO strategy. We offer both as a bundled growth package at competitive pricing.
