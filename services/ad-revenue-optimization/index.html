FAQ

AdSense & Revenue Questions Answered

Common questions about our publisher monetization services.

How much can I increase my AdSense revenue with BDM?

+

Most of our publisher clients see a 100–350% improvement in ad revenue within 3–6 months. The exact uplift depends on your current setup, traffic volume, niche and which networks we add. We start with an audit to give you a realistic revenue projection before you commit.

What is Google AdX and how is it different from AdSense?

+

Google AdX (Ad Exchange) is Google's premium programmatic marketplace — accessible only to high-quality publishers with significant traffic (usually 1M+ monthly page views). AdX connects your inventory to thousands of premium DSPs and brands bidding in real time, typically delivering CPMs 2–5x higher than AdSense. We help you access AdX through a Google Certified Publishing Partner.

Can I run Taboola/Outbrain alongside AdSense?

+

Yes — and this is one of the biggest revenue opportunities most publishers miss. Taboola and Outbrain native widgets sit below your article content and are completely separate from Google display ads. Running them together significantly increases your total RPM without any Google policy violation. We manage the entire integration and placement strategy.

What is header bidding and why does it matter?

+

Header bidding is an advanced programmatic technique where multiple ad networks bid for your impressions simultaneously — instead of sequentially (the old "waterfall" method). This means the highest bidder always wins your impression, dramatically increasing your average CPM. We implement Prebid.js header bidding as part of our Growth and Premium plans.

Will adding more ads hurt my Google rankings?

+

Not if done correctly. Google penalises sites with too many ads above the fold or that damage Core Web Vitals (CLS, LCP). We optimize your ad layout to maximize revenue while maintaining excellent Core Web Vitals scores and user experience — protecting your SEO rankings while growing your ad income.

My AdSense account was disabled — can you help?

+

We can audit your site for the specific policy violations that led to the ban, help you remediate the issues, and assist with the appeal process. We can also help you set up alternative monetization through Taboola, Outbrain or other networks while your AdSense appeal is in process.

What traffic volume do I need to get started?

+

For our Starter plan, we work with sites from 50K monthly page views upward. For Taboola and Outbrain integration, you typically need 100K+ monthly views. For Google AdX access, sites generally need 1M+ monthly page views. If you're below these thresholds, we'll focus on growing your traffic first before adding premium networks.

Do you offer white-label ad ops for agencies?

+

Yes. We provide full white-label ad operations services for digital agencies who manage publisher clients. All deliverables — setup, reports and dashboards — are unbranded and delivered under your agency name. Contact us for agency partnership details.
