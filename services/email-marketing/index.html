FAQ

Email Marketing Questions Answered

What email platform should I use — Mailchimp, Klaviyo or HubSpot?

+

It depends entirely on your business type. Klaviyo is the clear choice for eCommerce and D2C brands — its Shopify integration and revenue tracking are unmatched. HubSpot is best for B2B companies that need CRM + email integration and lead scoring. Mailchimp works well for SMBs, content creators and newsletters. ActiveCampaign suits complex multi-step automation funnels. We advise on the right platform for your situation before any work begins.

What open rate should I expect from my email campaigns?

+

Industry average open rates range from 20–25% for most sectors. Our clients consistently achieve 35–55% open rates on automated flows (welcome, cart recovery) and 28–42% on broadcast campaigns — significantly above industry benchmarks. Key factors include list health, subject line quality, sender reputation and segmentation accuracy — all areas we optimise from day one.

How do you ensure emails land in the inbox and not spam?

+

Deliverability is a combination of technical and content factors. We configure SPF, DKIM and DMARC DNS records for your sending domain, warm up new sending infrastructure gradually, maintain regular list hygiene (removing bounces and inactives), avoid spam-trigger language in subject lines and content, and run every campaign through spam testing tools before sending.

Do you write the email copy or do we need to provide content?

+

We provide full email copywriting as part of all our plans. Our email copywriters research your brand voice, audience and offers — then write subject lines, preview text and full email body copy designed for maximum open rates and conversions. You simply review and approve before anything goes out. If you have existing copy you prefer, we can edit and optimise it instead.

How do I grow my email list?

+

We implement a multi-channel list growth strategy — pop-up and exit-intent forms optimised for conversion, lead magnets (free guides, discounts, tools) that incentivise sign-ups, social media list-building campaigns and gated content. For B2B, we use targeted outreach and content upgrades. List growth strategy is included in our Growth and Enterprise plans.

What's the difference between a campaign and an automation flow?

+

A campaign (or broadcast) is a one-time email sent to a segment of your list on a specific date — like a newsletter or flash sale announcement. An automation flow (or sequence) is a series of emails triggered automatically by a subscriber's action — like signing up, abandoning a cart or making a purchase. Automation flows generate revenue 24/7 without ongoing management, which is why we always prioritise building them first.

Can you migrate my email list from one platform to another?

+

Yes — platform migrations are common and we manage them regularly. We export your subscriber data, segment information and suppression lists from your current platform, clean and format the data, and import it into your new platform while preserving all segmentation and compliance records. We also migrate and rebuild your existing automation flows in the new platform.

Do you offer email marketing for B2B cold outreach?

+

Yes. B2B cold email is a different discipline from permission-based email marketing — requiring different tools (Instantly, Apollo, Lemlist), different copywriting approaches (hyper-personalisation, research-first openers) and different deliverability infrastructure (separate cold email domains). We build and manage cold outreach sequences that generate qualified sales meetings for our B2B clients.
